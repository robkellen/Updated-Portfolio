Hello!

Welcome to my page. I love to code, but there is so much more to me than what I do. Please get to know a little about me before you find your way around...



I grew up in the NW suburbs of Chicago, but have been migrating West since 2007. I have called Arizona home since 2017 having moved from Wyoming where I began a promising career in hospitality in 2010. This year COVID-19 changed the world as we know it for everyone, and it unexpectedly provided me with an opportunity to embark on a new adventure into the world of coding. I only began this endeavor in July as a member of the University of Arizona's full stack bootcamp, and already know this path is what I will love dedicating my drive, creativity, and strong work ethic towards in the pursuit of never "working" another day in my life. Outside of coding I am a professional dad and husband, avid golfer, hibernating skier, world traveler, and a fiercely loyal follower of the Blackhawks, Cubs, Bears, Bulls, and Liverpool FC who enjoys kicking it with family and friends every chance I can.
